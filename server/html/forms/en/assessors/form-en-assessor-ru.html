Information and purpose

The study in which you are asked to participate focuses on subjective judgements of the difficulty of short English texts.

You will first be asked to fill in a short questionnaire about your language background. Then, you will be shown 8 randomly selected short texts in English in which, starting from the second sentence, half of every second word will be missing. Please fill in the missing halves and answer the questions below the text.

Please ensure that you are in a quiet environment. This survey will take about 45 minutes to complete.
